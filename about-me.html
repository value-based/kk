About Me

Kehinde Kamson is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Sweet Sensation Nigeria, as well as the president of the Restaurant & Food Services Proprietors Association of Nigeria, (REFSPAN) - the umbrella body for all Quick Service Restaurants in Nigeria.

She was born on the 14th of August 1961 in Lagos Nigeria to the Late Venerable Archdeacon Beniah Adeleke Adelaja, a well respected Clergyman and the former Principal of CMS Grammar School, and Late Omoba Evangeline Adebayo Adelaja, founder of Eva Adelaja Secondary School.

Kehinde attended Great Queen College where she wrote and passed her A-Levels, and went on to earn a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting from the University of Lagos. She is a Chartered Accountant and member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). She is also a graduate of the Lagos Business School. Kehinde interned and underwent professional training with PriceWaterCoopers before she went on to work in an oil service company as an accountant.

In a bid to spend quality time with her family, she quit her day job and ventured into a business - Sweet Sensation - that has become a hallmark of Quick Service Restaurants. With over twenty branches and 2,000 employees, Sweet Sensation is one of the most successful brands in Nigeria’s business community. Kehinde is currently expanding the business with the introduction of relevant in-demand food & lifestyle solutions, including farming to ensure Nigerians get a steady supply of organic ingredients.

She is married to Sir Olayinka Kamson, KJW and is a proud mother of five children and five grandchildren.

She believes that "Learning must be life long but the lessons of the earliest years are critical"

When she’s not swamped with work, she writes about interesting things, which she’d love to share with her audience.
