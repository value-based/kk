Writing

Yes, I love to write. I have put pen to paper and authored the book, Pots, Pans and Spoons; a memoir about my personal life and the story of how I founded and built Sweet Sensations from a backyard shed into one of the most profitable, popular, and respected chains of restaurants in Nigeria. The book contains my ideas, values, business philosophies, motivation, my mistakes, my daily routine, and my triumphs. At the core of my decision to write this book was the desire to inspire and encourage people to pursue their goals regardless of the obstacles that may stand in their way.

Pots, Pans and Spoons highlights my journey from self-discovery to the fulfilment of my God-given purpose as a pioneer and leader in the QSR business. Pots, Pans and Spoons is centered around my personal experiences, observing entrepreneurs around the world, important lessons from books, and courses I have attended.
