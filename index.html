About Me

Kehinde Kamson is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Sweet Sensation Nigeria, as well as the president of the Restaurant & Food Services Proprietors Association of Nigeria, (REFSPAN) - the umbrella body for all Quick Service Restaurants in Nigeria.

She was born on the 14th of August 1961 in Lagos Nigeria to the Late Venerable Archdeacon Beniah Adeleke Adelaja, a well respected Clergyman and the former Principal of CMS Grammar School, and Late Omoba Evangeline Adebayo Adelaja, founder of Eva Adelaja Secondary School...
