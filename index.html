About Me

Kehinde Kamson is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Sweet Sensation Nigeria, as well as the president of the Restaurant & Food Services Proprietors Association of Nigeria, (REFSPAN) - the umbrella body for all Quick Service Restaurants in Nigeria.
