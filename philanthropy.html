Philanthropy

Over the past 20 years of Sweet Sensationâ€™s existence, I have directed personal and business CSR resources aimed at supporting individuals and organizations across Nigeria by helping them cross financial, material, and intellectual barriers. I am a firm believer in imparting lives positively and contributing my own quota in developing society. My core area of interest has particularly been children and children-related institutions especially schools and those that serve challenged children. This interest prompted the Sweet Sensation Support Scheme (S.S.E.S) - an initiative that aims to provide support to children of low income families who may be unable to afford quality education due to inadequate financial resources. The educational initiative recognizes and rewards impressive academic performances, creative abilities, and leadership attributes in children.

The scheme which has served thousands of children in Lagos and beyond supports underprivileged students in various educational categories; private primary school, secondary school category (both private and public) and university category.

So far, over 50 students have benefitted from this scheme in various ways; tuition fees payments, exam fees, stationeries, textbooks, notebooks, school bags, uniforms, school sandals and other things. All the pioneer secondary school students have transited to the university, having aced their Senior Secondary Certificate Exams. The larger chunk of this category has graduated from the universities while a few are in their final lap in the system.
