Business

My decision to start Sweet Sensation was spurred by the difficulties I experienced equating home and work balance while working at PriceWaterhouseCoopers. On the one hand, I had my respectable profession, albeit stressful. On the other hand, was a somewhat demeaning pastry business. Confused about my future level of engagement, I sought the face of the Lord and got the go-ahead to continue with my cake and pastry business.

26 years later, Sweet Sensation has evolved beyond what I thought possible at the time I started, growing to represent the entrepreneurial, innovative and creative aspiration of the average Nigerian. My business which started at a very humble level has metamorphosed into a key brand in Nigeria, with over 20 outlets across the country. The brand currently boasts of several sub-brands, which include Corporate Cafeteria Service, Sweet Serve, Go Sensation, Cake Box, Sweet Scoop, Sweetheart Range of Breads, etc.
